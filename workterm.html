Tips for your workterm

Just remember 3 things

Relax

RELAX. You will have a wonderful time. Your team will be supportive and as long as you keep up with the basics, you will do well.

Be Reponsible

Don'take anything for granted. You will be expected to meet your quotas, but it won't be anything that is out of your capabilities. Manage your time well, be on time, and try your best to meet your expectations.

Make Use of Free Time

There will be times where you are given free time due to blockers. Make sure you make the most out of these times. Spend those time to study the codebase, what tickets are in staging, or maybe use the free time to teach your self some data structures or algorithms.
